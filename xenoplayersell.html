Your Ultimate Media & Manga Companion

XenoPlayer is a versatile, all-in-one media player and manga reader. It effortlessly handles both video files and images (including animated GIFs), automatically loading all supported media within the same directory as the opened file.

Beyond standard playback, XenoPlayer is packed with advanced features including AI-powered live manga translation, built-in online manga downloading, voice-controlled reading, and extensive file management tools. Everything is packaged into a single, easy-to-use application!

The Core Experience

Everything you need for seamless media playback and image viewing.

Folder-based Playback

Open a single file and instantly access a playlist of all media in that folder. XenoPlayer seamlessly transitions between playing videos and viewing images/GIFs, giving you a continuous, uninterrupted experience.

Favorites System

Keep track of your favorite files with ease. You can choose to play exclusively your favorites, or filter them out to watch only unfavorited content.

Live Thumbnails

No more guessing what a file contains. The built-in playlist dynamically generates previews for your videos and images on the fly, directly within the interface.

Next-Gen Manga Reading & Translation

Powered by AI, designed for comfort and accessibility.

Live AI Translation

Draw a box over foreign text in the Manga Viewer to automatically recognize and translate it to English (or other languages) using our built-in AI model.

Burn Translations

Optionally burn translated text directly into the image file using ImageMagick for permanent, seamless reading.

Voice Navigation

Sit back and control page-turning using voice commands. Just say "next", "back", or "previous" via speech recognition to turn pages hands-free.

Online Manga Integration

Browse popular tags, search, and download galleries directly from your favorite online sources using the built-in downloader.

Command Your Collection

Tools designed for massive media libraries.

Instant Move Targets

Instantly move files to pre-configured "Move Target" folders with a single click or hotkey. Perfect for sorting large batches of media.

Smart Duplicate Deletion

Automatically find and remove identical files in your library to save space and keep your collection clean.

Mass Renaming

Installation & Shortcuts

Get up and running in seconds.

🚀 Installation & Usage

  1. Download: Grab the latest XenoPlayer.exe (or installer) from the Releases page.
  2. Run: Double-click the executable to launch the app. No complex setup or dependencies required!
  3. Play: Drag and drop a file, or click "Load Folder/File" to start watching or reading.

*(Note: The AI translation model may download a small, one-time update on its very first run).*

⌨️ Main Player Shortcuts

  • Space: Play / Pause
  • Left / Right Arrow: Previous / Next media
  • Mouse Wheel Up / Down: Previous / Next media
  • + / -: Increase / Decrease video playback speed
  • Home / End: Jump to the start / end of the playlist
  • F2: Rename current or selected file(s)
  • F3: Move current or selected file(s) to a folder
  • Delete: Delete current or selected file(s)
  • Escape: Close application
  • Double-Click Video/Image: Toggle fullscreen

📖 Manga Viewer Shortcuts

  • Left / Right Arrow: Previous / Next page
  • Right-Click / Forward/Back Mouse Buttons: Navigate pages or clear selection boxes
  • Left-Click & Drag: Draw translation box
  • Voice Commands: Say "next", "forward", "back", or "previous" to turn pages (if Voice is enabled).
