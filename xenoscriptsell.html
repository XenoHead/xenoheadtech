An Editor, Not a Ghostwriter

We fiercely believe in protecting your creative voice. The AI in XenoScript is intentionally designed to never write your script for you.

  • No Auto-Complete: It will never hijack your story or write your scenes.
  • Constructive Critique: It will point out pacing issues, plot holes, or clunky dialogue.
  • Smart Suggestions: It acts like a professional script doctor, offering actionable feedback to help you elevate your own writing.

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

To ensure absolute privacy and give you full control over your usage, XenoScript uses a Bring Your Own Key model. Your script is never used to train our models. Simply plug in your own Google Gemini API key to securely activate your private story editor.
